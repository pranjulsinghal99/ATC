Services We Provide

HVAC & Controls

Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning(HVAC) is an important part any facility which helps to maintain the Conditioned air space & air quality.
We provide complete HVAC & Controls solutions including Supply, Installation & commissioning of Low Side HVAC, Cooling Tower, Spil/Window AC, Ductable units, etc. We also provide Annual Operation & Maintenance (O&M) Support of System.

FIRE

Fire Detection & suppression system is necessary for Safety of facility in case of any fire incident & to maintain Fire & Safty Regulations as per industrial norms. We provide complete solutions of Fire Detection & Suppression System including Supply, Installation & commissioning. We also deal in fire extinguishers, re-filling of cylinders & AMC.

CCTV

Apart from Physical security, Digital security by Closed Circuit Televison(CCTV) & Access Control System is very essential these days for any incident analysis & protection from intruders & theft. We provide complete solutions of Supply, Installation & Commissioning of Digital Security or CCTV system. We also deal in Annual Maintenance Contract as per requirement.

Industrial Valves & Sensors

Process control & optimisation is very much crucial for every industry i.e. manufacturing, pharmaceutical etc. We are able to support our clients with Industrial Sensors like proxemity sensor, temperature sensor etc & Valves like Solenoid Valves, Mechanical Valves etc based on Customer requirement.
